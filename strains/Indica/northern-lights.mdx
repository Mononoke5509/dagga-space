## Intro

**Northern Lights** _(aka. "**NL**")_ is an indica cannabis strain resulting from crossing the **Afghani** and **Thai** strains. NL is known for producing a strong euphoric mixed body-mind high, relaxing muscles and easing the mind. A puff from this legendary ðŸ”¥ strain puts you on a blissful path.

it up in a joint to baking some yummy snacks.
